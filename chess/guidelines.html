1. Platform


1.1. All the matches will be played on chess.com website or the chess.com android app for which the link is given below.
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.chess
1.2. Requests for using any other platform will not be entertained.

2. Eligibility of the Account


2.1. Use your own accounts, using someone else’s account to participate is not allowed, someone found doing so will be disqualified from the tournament immediately.
2.2. Only accounts with more than or equal to 100 games played is allowed.

3. Registration


3.1. Entry fee for this tournament is Rs 20.
3.1.1. Payment methods :- UPI/Paytm Wallet/PayPal.
3.1.2. No other payment methods will be accepted.
3.1.3. Payment details will be sent after filling the form.
3.2. To get yourself registered for the tournament fill up the form on the Sign-Up page of the website.
3.3. Registrations are open till 11:59pm of 11th March 2021.
3.3.1. Late submission fees – Rs 50.
3.3.2. After the bracket is made, no application will be accepted.

4. Structure of the tournament


4.1. Time control for every match will be 3+2 (3 mins + 2secs/move).
4.2. The winner of the tournament will be decided by Single or Double elimination format.
4.3. Final structure of the tournament will be decided according to the number of participants registered and will be announced 24 hours before the first match of the tournament starts.
4.4. For single elimination the semi-finals’ and final’s matches will be best of three matches.

5. Prize


5.1. The whole amount of money collected through registration fees will be given to the winner of this tournament (excluding the late registration fees)
5.2. Prize amount will be transferred to the same account from which the winner has paid the registration fees.

6. Code of Conduct


6.1. Before tournament starts
6.1.1. Every player is requested to become friends with every participant to avoid any problems before the match starts.
6.1.2. Please carefully take a look at the structure of the tournament after it is announced.
6.1.3. Please take a look at the date and time decided for the matches, and If you have any problems please talk it out with the morderator.
6.2. Before your match starts
6.2.1. Please be ready 10 mins before your match to avoid any fuss at the last moment.
6.2.2. Timing of the matches will get updated accordingly during the tournament.
6.2.3. Please be very sure about which opponent you are facing next and at what time.
6.2.4. The player who is going to send the game request, make sure that the time control of the match is correct.
6.2.5. Please make sure the matches are played on the chess.com live servers.
6.2.6. To change the timing of the match both players involved should agree with the new proposed time. (You cannot change the date of the match)
6.2.7. If both players do not come up with a time agreed by both of them, then the initial timing will be considered.
6.2.7.1. If there is no time left to play the match, then both the players will be considered to have forfeited the match.
6.3. During the match
6.3.1. Its is mandatory for every player to be on zoom call during their matches. Players can be asked to share their screen and turn on their camera if necessary. (Any player not ready to follow this rule is liable to be disqualified)
6.3.2. No one is allowed to get help from any other person/bot/chess engine during their match. (Any player found doing so will be immediately disqualified)

7. Terms and Conditions


7.1. In case of any dispute or confusion, the decision of the moderator will be considered the final decision.
7.2. The organisers have the right to disqualify any player at any time.
7.3. Playing in this tournament means you agree with our terms and conditions.
